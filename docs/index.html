Overview简介

AI setup for people who want outcomes, not terminal archaeology. 为重视结果的人准备的 AI 安装体验，而不是一堆难懂的终端命令。

TIAN turns a complicated stack of installers, environment variables, MCP configuration, and skill setup into a guided flow. It is aimed at business owners, professionals, and curious users who want practical AI help on their own machines. 「天」把原本复杂的安装器、环境变量、MCP 配置和技能包设置，整理成清晰的引导流程。它面向企业主、专业人士，以及想在自己电脑上真正用起来 AI 的普通用户。

macOS

Use a Homebrew-style one-line install command. It downloads TIAN, adds `tian-cli`, and starts the macOS setup flow.使用类似 Homebrew 的一行安装命令。它会下载 TIAN、添加 `tian-cli`，并启动 macOS 安装流程。

What is TIAN?TIAN 是什么？

TIAN is a project that helps users install and configure AI assistants, MCP integrations, and skills on their own computers.TIAN 是一个帮助用户在自己电脑上安装和配置 AI 助手、MCP 集成和技能包的项目。

Who is TIAN for?TIAN 适合谁？

It is designed for non-technical users, business operators, professionals, and developers who want a faster setup path.它适合希望更快完成 AI 配置的非技术用户、业务运营者、专业人士和开发者。
