# Tian — Talking Is All You Need

> Set up AI on your computer in minutes. No coding. No technical background. Just follow the steps.

---

## What is Tian?

Tian is a simple setup tool that helps **anyone** — even with zero knowledge of AI or software — get powerful AI assistants running on their Windows computer. Whether you want help with daily tasks or want to automate parts of your business, Tian makes it easy.

You don't need to know what "terminal", "Python", or "API" means. If you can install an app on Windows, you can use Tian.

---

## What can you do with it?

- **Chat with AI** to get help with emails, documents, research, spreadsheets, and more
- **Set up MCP servers** — these are plugins that let your AI connect to tools like files, calendars, email, web search, and business software
- **Install skills** tailored to your daily workflow or business needs
- **Switch AI backends** based on your preference or budget — Claude, Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, and more

---

## Who is this for?

- Business owners who want AI to help with day-to-day operations
- Professionals who want to save time on repetitive tasks
- Anyone curious about AI who has been held back by the complexity of setup

---

## Requirements

- A Windows computer (Windows 10 or later)
- Basic ability to click, type, and follow instructions
- An internet connection
- An API key from your chosen AI provider (step-by-step instructions included)

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1 — Download Tian

Click the green **Code** button at the top of this page, then click **Download ZIP**. Unzip the folder somewhere easy to find, like your Desktop.

### Step 2 — Run the Setup Wizard

Inside the folder, double-click `setup.bat`. A simple window will open and guide you through:

- Choosing your AI backend (Claude, Codex, or others)
- Entering your API key
- Selecting which skills and MCP tools to install

### Step 3 — Start Talking

Once setup is complete, launch the app and start chatting. Ask it anything.

## Available Skills & MCP Tools

Skills extend what your AI can do. Install only what you need:

**Daily Use**
- Email drafting and summarization
- Calendar and scheduling assistant
- Web search and research
- Document and PDF reader

**Business**
- Customer support templates
- Invoice and spreadsheet automation
- Meeting notes and action items
- CRM and data entry helpers

More skills can be added from the community or built for your specific business.
